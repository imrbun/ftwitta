FAQ

Quick Answers to some Common Questions

Explore helpful answers to common questions about booking courts, facilities, and playing at our venue.

Do I need to book a table in advance?


While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially during peak hours.

What are your operating hours?


Your venue is open daily. Please check our opening hours on the Contact page for the latest schedule.

Do you provide rackets and balls?


Yes, rackets and balls are available for renting at the venue. You are also welcome to bring your own equipment if you prefer.

How long can I rent a table?


Tables are typically rented by the hour, but you may extend your session depending on availability.

Can we host events or tournaments here?


Yes, our venue can accommodate friendly matches, group gatherings, and small tournaments. Please contact us for arrangements.

Is there parking available at the venue?


Yes, parking is available nearby for visitors. Please check with our staff if you need assistance finding parking.
