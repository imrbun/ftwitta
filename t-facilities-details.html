The perfect environment to push your limits and grow.

The Outdoor Strength & Conditioning Zone offers a dynamic and open-air training environment designed for athletes, bodybuilders, and fitness enthusiasts who want to push their limits outside of traditional gym walls. With state-of-the-art outdoor equipment and functional training areas, this facility is built to enhance strength, power, endurance, and mobility—all while enjoying fresh air and natural surroundings.

Key Features & Equipment

  • Outdoor Olympic Lifting Platforms – Durable, all-weather platforms for heavy lifts and explosive strength training.
  • Heavy-Duty Power Racks – Adjustable racks for squats, bench presses, and other compound exercises.
  • Turf Training Area – Perfect for sled pushes, tire flips, and agility drills.
  • Strongman Equipment – Including yoke carries, atlas stones, sandbags, and farmer’s carry handles.
  • Rope Climb & Pull-Up Rigs – Designed for bodyweight strength and endurance challenges.
  • Battle Ropes & Sledgehammers – High-intensity conditioning tools for power and stamina.
