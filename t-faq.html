FAQ

We’ve compiled answers to the most common questions.

We have gathered answers to frequently asked questions regarding our gym, facilities, and programs.

What are your gym’s hours of operation?


Our facility is open Monday–Friday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM and Saturday–Sunday from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Do I need a membership to use the gym?


Yes, we offer various membership plans to suit different fitness needs. However, we do offer day passes and trial sessions for first-time visitors.

Can I tour the facility before signing up?


Absolutely! We offer guided tours of our gym and training zones. Just stop by or schedule a tour in advance.

What types of memberships do you offer?


We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual memberships with different access levels, including gym-only, all-access, and specialized training packages.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?


Yes, memberships can be frozen for medical or travel reasons. Cancellation policies vary based on the membership plan. Contact our front desk for details.

Do you offer discounts for students or families?


Yes! We have special pricing for students, families, and corporate groups. Check with our membership team for the latest offers.

Do you offer personal training?


Yes! We have certified personal trainers who can help you with customized fitness plans based on your goals.

Do you offer workshops and training camps?


Yes! We host specialized athlete training camps and recovery workshops. Keep an eye on our schedule for upcoming programs.
